HyperWhisper is fully open source and free. **All local, on-device transcription and every local model are free and unlimited** — there are no daily limits and no cap on downloads. The only paid offering is **HyperWhisper Cloud**, an optional hosted transcription service billed with prepaid credits.

## What's free vs paid

| | Local (free for everyone) | HyperWhisper Cloud (paid) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| On-device transcription | **Unlimited** | — |
| Local model downloads | **Unlimited** | — |
| BYOK providers (OpenAI, Groq, Deepgram, etc.) | ✓ | — |
| Auto-paste, custom modes, vocabulary | ✓ | ✓ |
| Hosted (cloud) transcription | — | Prepaid **credits** (pay-as-you-go) |
| Cost | Free | Credits, purchased as needed |

## What are HyperWhisper Cloud credits?

Credits are the currency for using HyperWhisper's hosted transcription service (HyperWhisper Cloud). When you transcribe audio through HyperWhisper Cloud, credits are deducted from your balance based on audio length — approximately 6.3 credits per minute of audio.

At the rate of 5,000 credits for $5 ($0.001 per credit), and 6.3 credits consumed per minute, that works out to roughly **$0.006 per minute** of cloud transcription. You only spend credits when you choose a HyperWhisper Cloud provider; BYOK providers (where you supply your own API key) do not consume credits.

**Trial users** start with 150 free credits — enough to try HyperWhisper Cloud for around 23 minutes before needing to top up.

**Pro users** receive 5,000 credits (~$5 value) bundled with their license purchase. Additional credits can be purchased separately from your billing dashboard when your balance runs low.

<Note>
  Credits are tied to your account, not to a specific device. On trial, your credits are tracked by a device identifier assigned to your machine — rotating to a different device on trial does not grant a fresh credit balance.
</Note>

## Local transcription is unlimited

There are no limits on local, on-device use. Record for as long as you like, as often as you like, and download as many local models as you want — all free, with no account required. HyperWhisper is open source, so the local path has no paywall of any kind.

You only spend money if you opt into **HyperWhisper Cloud** for hosted transcription, which is billed with prepaid credits (above).

## Purchasing HyperWhisper Cloud credits

Open the **HyperWhisper Cloud** panel from the app sidebar (or **Settings → License**) to add credits. Clicking it opens the checkout page in your browser.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open HyperWhisper Cloud in the app">
    From the menu bar (macOS) or tray (Windows), open **Settings → License**, or click the **HyperWhisper Cloud** button in the sidebar.

    Alternatively, visit [hyperwhisper.com/checkout](https://www.hyperwhisper.com/checkout) directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete checkout">
    You'll be taken to a Stripe Checkout page. Enter your email and payment details. Promotion codes are supported — paste one in the discount field if you have one. The license is a one-time purchase (not a subscription).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive your license key">
    After payment, HyperWhisper emails your license key to the address you entered. You'll also see a confirmation page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Activating a Pro license

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → License">
    From the menu bar (macOS) or tray (Windows), open **Settings → License**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Paste your license key">
    Paste the key from your purchase confirmation email into the license key field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Activate">
    HyperWhisper validates the key online and unlocks Pro features immediately. Your bundled 5,000 cloud credits are credited to your account at the same time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Activation requires a one-time internet connection. After it completes, you can keep using HyperWhisper offline (with local models).

## Managing your billing

Once your license is active, a **Manage Billing** button appears in **Settings → License** (on both macOS and Windows). Clicking it opens your billing dashboard at [hyperwhisper.com/user](https://www.hyperwhisper.com/user), where you can:

- Check your remaining cloud credit balance
- View purchase history
- Buy additional cloud credits when your balance runs low
- Update your payment method

<Note>
  The Manage Billing button is only visible when a Pro license is active. Trial users can visit [hyperwhisper.com/user](https://www.hyperwhisper.com/user) directly after creating an account.
</Note>

## Using your license on multiple machines

A single license is intended for personal use across the machines you own — for example, a desktop and a laptop. There's no rigid per-device install limit, but the license is tied to your account and is not for redistribution.

If you need to use HyperWhisper on a team or across many machines, contact [support@hyperwhisper.com](mailto:support@hyperwhisper.com) about volume licensing.

## Moving to a new Mac or PC

If you're replacing a device or selling an old one:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Deactivate on the old machine (recommended)">
    Open **Settings → License → Deactivate** on the machine you're leaving. This frees the activation slot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install HyperWhisper on the new machine">
    Download from [hyperwhisper.com](https://www.hyperwhisper.com).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate with the same key">
    Paste your license key in **Settings → License** on the new machine.
  </Step>
</Steps>

If you no longer have access to the old machine (lost, stolen, wiped), email [support@hyperwhisper.com](mailto:support@hyperwhisper.com) with your license key and we'll free the slot manually.

## Lost your license key?

License keys are sent by email at the time of purchase. If you can't find yours, search your inbox for "HyperWhisper" or "Stripe" (our payment processor). You can also retrieve your key by visiting [hyperwhisper.com/user](https://www.hyperwhisper.com/user) and signing in with the email you used to purchase. If you still can't find it, email [support@hyperwhisper.com](mailto:support@hyperwhisper.com) with your purchase email address.

## Localization Maintenance

When `app/macos/hyperwhisper/Localizations/Base.lproj/Localizable.strings` changes, use the `localisation-syncer` skill to sync and update ALL sibling locale folders in `app/macos/hyperwhisper/Localizations/`.

There are 39 non-Base locales: ar, bg, ca, cs, da, de, el, es, et, fi, fr, he, hi, hr, hu, id, is, it, ja, ko, lt, lv, ms, nb, nl, pl, pt, ro, ru, sk, sl, sr, sv, th, tr, uk, vi, zh-Hans, zh-Hant.
